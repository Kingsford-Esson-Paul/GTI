Planting Golden Seeds
on Fertile Grounds


Golden Tintona Investment (GTI) is a multinational industry company. Founded in August 2016, GTI Holding has grown to enrich its portfolio across various spectrums. With global offices in New Jersey (USA) and Accra (Ghana), and regional offices in the Dubai (UAE), China, Sao Tome & Principe, Liberia, and the Democratic Republic of Congo, the Holding’s subsidiaries have left indelible golden footprints on continents, through cutting edge products and services.

Golden Harvests

Yearly, GTI Holding reaps bountiful golden harvests as our unwavering focus is on sectors critical to human existence and development. These sectors include the Health, Education, Information Technology, Communication, Energy, Natural Resource, Finance, Human Capital Development and Agricultural Sectors.
